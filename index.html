Manifesto

We ♥ design meet-ups to mingle and network, to inspire and being inspired. We also agree with Plato that "you can discover more about a person in an hour of play than in a year of conversation." Hence we run DestrActions, a playground for anybody interested in design to get their hands dirty, meet others and learn from them and simply a break from your long-term project routine.

If you think you have an interesting challenge, please get in touch as we are happy to run it.
